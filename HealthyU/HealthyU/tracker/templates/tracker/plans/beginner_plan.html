Program Overview

Perfect for those just starting their fitness journey! This 4-week beginner program focuses on building a solid foundation, establishing healthy habits, and gradually increasing your fitness level. No prior experience needed - we'll guide you every step of the way.

4-Week Program

1

Week 1: Getting Started

Introduction to basic movements and building consistency. Focus on proper form and breathing.

  • Monday: 15-min Walk + Light Stretching
  • Tuesday: Basic Bodyweight Exercises (10 reps each)
  • Wednesday: Rest or Gentle Yoga
  • Thursday: 20-min Walk
  • Friday: Bodyweight Circuit (2 rounds)
  • Weekend: Active Recovery (Walking, Stretching)
2

Week 2: Building Momentum

Increase duration and add variety. Start feeling more comfortable with exercises.

  • Monday: 20-min Walk + Stretching
  • Tuesday: Bodyweight Exercises (12 reps each)
  • Wednesday: Yoga or Pilates (20 min)
  • Thursday: 25-min Walk or Light Jog
  • Friday: Full Body Circuit (3 rounds)
  • Weekend: Fun Activity (Dancing, Swimming)
3

Week 3: Increasing Intensity

Add more challenging variations and increase workout time.

  • Monday: 25-min Brisk Walk
  • Tuesday: Strength Training (15 reps, 3 sets)
  • Wednesday: Yoga Flow (25 min)
  • Thursday: Interval Walking (30 min)
  • Friday: Total Body Workout
  • Weekend: Active Rest + Stretching
4

Week 4: Establishing Routine

Solidify your routine and prepare for the next level.

  • Monday: 30-min Walk/Jog
  • Tuesday: Full Body Strength (3 sets)
  • Wednesday: Power Yoga (30 min)
  • Thursday: Cardio Mix (30 min)
  • Friday: Complete Workout Routine
  • Weekend: Celebration Activity!

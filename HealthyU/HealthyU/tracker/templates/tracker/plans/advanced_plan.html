Program Overview

For experienced athletes ready to push their limits! This intense 8-week program combines strength, power, endurance, and agility training. Designed for those with a solid fitness foundation looking to achieve peak performance and maximum results.

8-Week Elite Program

1

Weeks 1-2: Strength Foundation

Heavy compound lifts and progressive overload. Build maximum strength.

  • Monday: Heavy Squats & Deadlifts (5x5)
  • Tuesday: Upper Body Power (Bench, Rows)
  • Wednesday: HIIT Cardio + Core
  • Thursday: Olympic Lifts (Cleans, Snatches)
  • Friday: Hypertrophy Day (High Volume)
  • Saturday: Endurance Training (60 min)
  • Sunday: Active Recovery + Mobility
2

Weeks 3-4: Power Development

Explosive movements and plyometrics. Increase power output.

  • Monday: Plyometric Training + Sprints
  • Tuesday: Max Effort Upper Body
  • Wednesday: Metabolic Conditioning
  • Thursday: Dynamic Lower Body
  • Friday: Full Body Power Circuit
  • Saturday: Long Distance Run/Bike
  • Sunday: Yoga + Stretching
3

Weeks 5-6: Hypertrophy Phase

High volume training for muscle growth and definition.

  • Monday: Chest & Triceps (4x12)
  • Tuesday: Back & Biceps (4x12)
  • Wednesday: Legs (High Volume)
  • Thursday: Shoulders & Core
  • Friday: Full Body Pump
  • Saturday: CrossFit Style WOD
  • Sunday: Recovery + Sauna
4

Weeks 7-8: Peak Performance

Combine all elements for maximum athletic performance.

  • Monday: Total Body Strength
  • Tuesday: Speed & Agility Training
  • Wednesday: Endurance Challenge
  • Thursday: Power & Explosiveness
  • Friday: Competition Simulation
  • Saturday: Beast Mode Workout
  • Sunday: Complete Rest

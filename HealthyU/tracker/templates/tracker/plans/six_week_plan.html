Program Overview

Transform your body and mind with our comprehensive 6-week program. This intermediate-level plan combines strength training, cardio, and flexibility work to help you achieve balanced fitness. Each week builds upon the last, progressively challenging you while allowing adequate recovery.

Weekly Breakdown

1

Week 1-2: Foundation Building

Focus on proper form and building endurance. Includes full-body workouts 4 days/week with moderate intensity cardio.

  • Monday: Upper Body Strength
  • Tuesday: Cardio & Core
  • Wednesday: Rest or Light Yoga
  • Thursday: Lower Body Strength
  • Friday: Full Body Circuit
  • Weekend: Active Recovery
2

Week 3-4: Intensity Increase

Increase weights and intensity. Add HIIT sessions and compound movements for maximum results.

  • Monday: Push Day (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
  • Tuesday: HIIT Cardio
  • Wednesday: Pull Day (Back, Biceps)
  • Thursday: Leg Day
  • Friday: Full Body Power
  • Weekend: Yoga & Stretching
3

Week 5-6: Peak Performance

Push your limits with advanced techniques. Focus on muscle definition and cardiovascular endurance.

  • Monday: Strength & Power
  • Tuesday: Cardio Intervals
  • Wednesday: Hypertrophy Training
  • Thursday: Athletic Performance
  • Friday: Total Body Burn
  • Weekend: Active Recovery & Assessment
